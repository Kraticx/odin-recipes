Cheeseburger

Description:

A hamburger with a slice of melted cheese on top of the meat patty. (Assortments can sometimes vary.)

Ingredients:

  • Hamburger Bun
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato
  • Onions (optional)
  • American Cheese (or whatever kind of cheese you want.)
  • Lettuce

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. divide the ground beef into four equal portions and shape them into patties
  3. Place the patties on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160Â°F.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of grilling.
  5. Remove the patties from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill.
  7. Assemble the cheeseburgers with your desired condiments and toppings. (i.e lettuce, tomato, ketchup, etc.)
