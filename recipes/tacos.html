Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef, turkey, or chicken
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 (11/4 ounce) packet of taco seasoning or 7 teaspoons of taco seasoning.
  • 3/4 cuo of water or beer (if desired)
  • 8 fresh cilantro stems, minced (optional)
  • 1 pinch of sugar (optional)
  • Toppings can include: lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, sour cream, etc.

Steps:

  1. Cook meat, onion, garlic and cilantro stirring often until meat is no longer pink. Drain fat.
  2. Add seasoning and 3/4 cup water or beer to meat mixture and stir to blend. You could also use half beer and half water.
  3. Simmer until liquid has evaporated.
  4. Serve with desired toppings. Get Creative!
