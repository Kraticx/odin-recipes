Pizza

Description:

A dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combinations of ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella (or other cheeses)
  • Pepperoni or sometimes other meats such as sausage.

Steps:

  1. Prepare Dough:Mix Ingredients: Combine flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and salt. Knead: Work the dough until smooth, then let it rise.
  2. Roll out Dough:Flour Surface: Roll the dough into desired thickness. Shape: Create a round or rectangular base.
  3. Add Sauce and Toppings:Spread Sauce: Tomato sauce or olive oil for the base. Cheese and More: Layer with mozzarella and favorite toppings.
  4. Bake:Preheat Oven: Ensure it's hot (around 475°F or 245°C). Cook Time: Bake until crust is golden and cheese melts (12-15 minutes).
  5. Enjoy Fresh:Slice and Serve: Let it cool slightly before cutting. Garnish: Fresh herbs or a drizzle of olive oil for a finishing touch.
